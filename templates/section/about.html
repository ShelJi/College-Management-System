About Us

Noorul Islam College of Arts and Science NICAS

Noorul Islam College of Arts and Science (NICAS), located in Kumaracoil, Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, is a private institute established in 2001, affiliated with Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, and offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses.

  • Establishment:

    NICAS was founded in 2001 by the Noorul Islam Educational Trust.

  • Affiliation:

    It is affiliated with Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli.

  • Accreditation:

    The college has been accredited by NAAC with a 'B' grade and a CGPA of 2.66.
